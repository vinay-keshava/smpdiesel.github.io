SMP Diesel's Privacy Policy

At SMP Diesel, we value our customers’ trust and respect their right to privacy. We are committed to protecting the personal information that you share with us.

1. Protection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from our customers during interactions, such as name, contact details, and other relevant information. We assure you that this information will not be shared with any third-party individuals or organizations without your explicit consent. We respect your satisfaction and strive to maintain the highest level of confidentiality.

2. Website Privacy

Our website is built using open-source software called Hugo. We do not store any information about our website visitors. We do not collect or retain any personal data, such as IP addresses, browsing history, or cookies. Your visit to our website is completely anonymous.

By using our website and services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood our privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Note: This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will post any changes on this page, so please check back regularly to stay informed.
