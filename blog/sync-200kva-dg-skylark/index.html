First Mahindra Dealer to Synchronize Two 200kVA Diesel Generators in South India in CPCB 4+

SMP Diesel has achieved a groundbreaking feat by becoming the first Mahindra Sales Dealer in South India to successfully synchronize two 200kVA diesel generators in CPCB 4+ at the prestigious Skylark Estate Apartments,Hoodi Bengaluru. This remarkable achievement sets a new benchmark for the industry and showcases SMP Diesel’s technical expertise.

We would like to extend our heartfelt gratitude to VijayKumar, the Mahindra Team, and our vendors who played a crucial role in helping us achieve this feat. Their expertise, support, and cooperation were instrumental in making this project a success.

We are proud of what we have accomplished and couldn’t have done it without the help and support of so many people. Thank you to everyone who contributed to this achievement! SMP Diesel is committed to providing customized solutions to meet the unique needs of its customers, and this achievement is a testament to our expertise and dedication.
